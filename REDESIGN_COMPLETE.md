## 🎨 Design System Summary

### Material 3 (Material You)
- ✅ 24dp corner radius on all cards
- ✅ 2dp elevation for subtle shadows
- ✅ Tonal buttons with colored backgrounds
- ✅ 56-64dp button heights
- ✅ 20-24dp padding inside cards
- ✅ Google's color palette
- ✅ Bold, friendly typography

### Color Palette
```
🔵 Blue:   #4285F4 (Primary actions)
🔴 Red:    #EA4335 (Danger/Lock)
🟡 Yellow: #FBBC04 (Warning)
🟢 Green:  #34A853 (Success/Unlock)
```
